America’s 1 million homeless Veterans are asking America’s Educators for help. Our corporation is dedicated to providing what America’s homeless Veterans need to reclaim our lives and the dreams we set aside to serve America in the military.

As an extension of the help our corporation provides to homeless Veterans, we have developed an associated program, “The Homeless American veterans Tuition Aid Program”, whose purpose is to help homeless Veterans gain “marketable job skills.”

The immediate goal of our Tuition Aid Program is to provide homeless Veterans with instruction in the basic building trade skills (carpentry, plumbing, etc.,) that are needed to successfully participate in our “self-help housing renovation initiative.” (Click on “Pilot Project.”) Our long range goal is to provide homeless Veterans with the widest possible range of educational opportunities, including a college education. (Those Veterans who have successfully participated in our building renovation initiative, are then eligible for education in any profession they choose.)

Ours is a “ Direct Payment Grant Program”, in which our Corporate Sponsors make Tax Deductible donations of $10,000 or more, in the name of our non-profit, 501 (c) (3) corporation, The Homeless American Veterans Self-Help Initiative, Inc., directly to the participating school, in advance of enrollment. Our corporate sponsors are asking each school, as a condition of participation in the grant program, to donate to America’s homeless Veterans, an equivalent, tax deductible value of classroom instruction time, for each dollar of tuition aid provided by the sponsoring corporation.

(That’s like giving homeless Veterans a 50% discount.) To receive a grant, each school must sign our “ Letter of Intent To Participate.”

All other related expenses, including transportation, supplies, meals, etc., will be paid directly to the Veteran/Student by our corporation. All Veteran/Students have been pre-qualified and certified by our corporation as eligible to participate.

The help of America’s Educators is vital to the success of our mission. By participating in “The Homeless American Veterans Tuition Aid Program”, you are helping to restore hope and self-respect to those who have guaranteed America’s freedom.
