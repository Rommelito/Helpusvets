“No American Veteran Should Ever Be Homeless.”
A Report From The Frontlines
Part III.

“Our solution to “the problem of homelessness among America’s Armed Forces Veterans” is based entirely on self-help and self-reliance. Our corporate motto, our corporate mandate and our corporate services agenda make self-help the main focus of our effort.

Presented here are the corporate documents filed with the State of New York and the Internal Revenue Service, upon which our status as a non-profit, tax exempt, 501 (c) (3) Charitable Organization of America’s Armed Forces Veterans are based.

The whole objective of our corporation and its promotions is to help America’s homeless Veterans, by providing re-education in the building renovation trades, and then, providing abandoned residential properties, that have been donated to our cause, or bought with funds provided by our swimming event promotions, so that homeless Veterans can renovate them for themselves and their families, who will then become the new owner/occupants.”

Our aim
 is the restoration of hope and self-respect
 to those who have
