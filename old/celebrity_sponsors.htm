“Operation Celebrity Autograph”

An open letter to America’s celebrities from America’s homeless Veterans.

Dear Celebrity America,
America’s Armed Forces Veterans are asking for your help. We are seeking sponsors for our “ International Swimming Event Promotion ”, featuring our homeless, disabled Vietnam Veteran Swimmer, who will attempt to become, The World’s First Disabled Person To Swim Around Manhattan Island , in support of our Corporate Mandate, help for America’s 1 million homeless Veterans.
Our promotional event is intended to bring America’s attention to “the problem of homelessness among America’s Armed Forces Veterans.”


Our corporation, The Homeless American Veterans Self-Help Initiative, Inc., is a non-profit, 501 (c) (3) Charitable Organization of America’s Armed Forces Veterans. To verify, toll free, call I.R.S. Hotline, 1.877.829.5500. We are also registered with the NY State Attorney General’s Office, Charities Bureau, 2212.416.8400. Our Certificates of Legal Authority are available for your inspection, upon request. (E-mail, HelpUsVets @Yahoo.com .)


Our mission, as outlined on our website, www. HelpUsVets.org , is to help America’s homeless Veterans obtain abandoned, tax delinquent residential properties, with funds provided by our corporation’s promotional activities, and renovating them, utilizing the labor and expertise of homeless Veterans , who will then take possession of the newly renovated property, with their families, as the new owner/occupants .


We are asking all of America’s celebrities, professional athletes, sports teams, entertainers, artists, and the rich and famous, to Help Us Vets, by joining our celebrity autograph project, “ Operation Celebrity Autograph .”
Your participation will help begin a new life for a homeless Veteran and his family, just by adding your autograph , along with other celebrities, to our “Vietnam Veterans Commemorative Poster, entitled, “A Hero’s Heaven.”


Each poster is imprinted with the name of a KIA (Killed In Action) Vietnam Veteran, who gave his life in service to America. It has been hand-rubbed directly from “The National Vietnam Veterans Memorial Wall”, in Washington, D.C., by the first group of homeless Veterans who will benefit from your help. Each poster is embossed with our Official Corporate Seal and comes with a Certificate of Authenticity. Our objective is to auction the professionally framed, mounted and autographed posters to help raise the funds to acquire and renovate our “ Pilot Project ”, as outlined on our website.

The successful completion of our Pilot Project will Help Us Vets expand our “homeless Veterans self-help housing initiative” to other cities across America, to help many other homeless Veterans.
Please E-mail, HelpUsVets @Yahoo.com to arrange a convenient time and place for us to meet and discuss the details of your participation in “ Operation Celebrity Autograph .”

Your help is vital to the success of our mission.
Thank you for your support of America’s homeless Veterans.
James D. Sewell, President,
The Homeless American Veterans Self-Help Initiative, Inc.
