“…America’s 1 million homeless Veterans
are asking for your help.”

Our appeal for help is to the American people and to Corporate America, who are the beneficiaries of the sacrifices that America’s Veterans have made countless times, in countless unknown places, all over the world.

We are also making an appeal to “The Worldwide Fraternity of America’s Armed Forces Veterans” for their support of our effort to help homeless Veterans.

 

Your help is already putting homeless Veterans to work. Your poster, “A Hero’s Heaven”, is imprinted with the name of a KIA (Killed In Action) Vietnam Veteran, who gave his life in service to America. It has been hand-rubbed directly from The National Vietnam Veterans Memorial Wall in Washington, D.C. by the first group of homeless Veterans who will benefit from your help. Your poster donations are how we finance our Corporate Services Agenda For Homeless Veterans.
