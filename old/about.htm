Our corporation is a non-profit, tax exempt, self-help effort, created by homeless New York Veterans, to serve the housing and re-education needs of America’s homeless Veterans, through a program of abandoned property acquisition, sweat equity renovations and re-education.

We are a non-profit, 501 (c) (3) Charitable Organization of America’s Armed Forces Veterans. To verify, toll free, call I.R.S. Hotline, 1.877.829.5500. We are also registered with the N.Y. State Attorney General’s Office, Charities Bureau, 212.416.8400. Our Certificates of Legal Authority are available for your inspection, upon request. (E-mail, HelpUsVets@Yahoo.com)

“Self-help is the only real answer.”

Our purpose is to acquire homes for America’s homeless Veterans, from the existing stock of abandoned, tax delinquent residential properties that exist in every American city, with funds provided by our corporation’s promotional activities, and through City, State and Federal programs, and renovating them, utilizing the labor and expertise of America’s homeless Veterans. As part of our Corporate Mandate, “help for America’s ½ million homeless Veterans”, we will renovate in each project, an appropriate number of “accessible housing units” for disabled homeless Veterans.

The long range goal of our corporation is the creation of full-time employment opportunities for every homeless Veteran, through the establishment of business ownership opportunities for those Veterans who have successfully completed our re-education initiative.

“You re-build it … you own it, is the only real solution.”

The aim of our self-help building renovation program is for each participant who has given his labor and expertise to the completion of a renovation project to take possession of the newly renovated property, with the other participating “construction tam members”, as the new owner/occupants, each owning an equal share.

By the completion of a renovation project, the formerly homeless Veteran has gained the education and the hands-on experience that are necessary to obtain full-time employment as professional craftsmen, apprentices, and/or skilled laborers, and, the stability of a permanent home from which to pursue an other goal.

Our Corporate Mandate

The Homeless American Veterans Self-Help Initiative, Inc. was born from the desire of homeless Veterans to reclaim our lives and the dreams we set aside to serve America in the military. Our corporation is dedicated to providing what America’s homeless Veterans need to reclaim their lives and their dreams.As homeless Veterans ourselves, we have learned that the most effective way to help a homeless Veteran is through providing what is necessary for him to help himself. It is self-help and personal achievement that restore hope and self-respect.

Our aim is the restoration of hope and self-respect to those who have
Guaranteed America’s Freedom.
